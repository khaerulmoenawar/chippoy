To My Love

My Dearest Chippy,

Do you remember how the sunlight filtered through the trees at GBK that first afternoon? I do. I remember thinking how your laughter sounded like my new favorite song, and how your eyes held galaxies I wanted to explore forever. That day, November 2nd, 2023, wasn't just a date - it was the day my soul recognized its home.

My love, these two years with you have been the most beautiful education in what love truly means. You've taught me that love isn't always grand gestures - sometimes it's in the way you waited patiently while I struggled to find my path, or how your eyes still light up when you see me even after all this time. It's in the ruined film rolls that became our favorite story, and the rainy days at Bogor that felt like blessings because we were together.

I've fallen in love with you in so many ways - in the quiet moments at Jiwan Cafe where our souls spoke without words, in the way you hold my hand during movies like it's the most natural thing in the world, and in the strength you showed while chasing your dreams abroad. Each version of you I discover becomes more precious than the last.

When I think about our journey - the passionate fire of our first year that tempered into the steady flame of our second - I'm overwhelmed with gratitude. Thank you for choosing us even when it was hard. Thank you for seeing the man I could become and loving the man I am. Thank you for being my safe harbor during storms of uncertainty and my brightest celebration in moments of joy.

You are my greatest adventure, my most beautiful surprise, and my heart's truest companion. The distance we've endured only proved what I've always known - our connection transcends time and space. You're the first thought that greets me each morning and the sweet dream that visits me each night.

As we celebrate two years of us, I want you to know that my love for you has deepened in ways I never thought possible. It's grown roots that no storm can shake, and wings that help us soar through every challenge. I love you not only for who you are, but for who I become when I'm with you.

Forever yours, in this lifetime and every other,

With all that I am,

And all that I hope to be,
